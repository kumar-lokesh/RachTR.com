RachTR-Floor-Screed-EP-81


DESCRIPTION :

RachTR EP81 is three components, self levelling heavy duty industrial flooring screed.


PRODUCT BENEFITS :

  1. High strength, heavy duty screed
  2. Good abrasion and impact resistance
  3. High durability and chemically resistant
  4. Good anti-skid properties

METHOD OF APPLICATION :

  1. The product may be applied by a serrated/ notch trowel to the required thickness.
  2. The entire mixed material should be poured onto the primed surface and spread slowly and evenly.
  3. To ensure proper levelling and appearance avoid overspreading.
  4. Tamp the laid material with a wooden float to ensure compactness. Finish the surface with a suitable steel trowel.
  5. An overlap of 50% with adjacent paths is recommended.
  6. To avoid roller marks prevent over rolling of the coating.
  7. The coverage & levelling would vary significantly based on the nature & levelling of the concrete surface.
  8. Freshly applied material should be protected from damp, condensation and water for at least 24 hours.
  9. At low temperatures, the chemical reactions are slowed down; this lengthens the pot life, open time & curing times.
  10. High temperatures speed up the chemical reactions thus the time frames mentioned above are shortened accordingly
