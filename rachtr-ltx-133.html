RachTR LTX-133


DESCRIPTION :

LTX133 is acrylic latex mixed into TSA ST or TSA PR, undiluted or diluted 1:1 with water.


RECOMMENDATIONS :

    TSA ST or PR mixed with LTX133 must never be used for:

  1. Installing marble or natural stone subject to efflorescence or staining from moisture
  2. Installing on metal, rubber, PVC, and linoleum surfaces
  3. At temperatures lower than +5°C or higher than +45°C
  4. In dry, hot climates, the open time of and adhesive made by mixing LTX133 into TSA ST or TSA PR is shorter. A skin may form on the surface, which must then be removed and fresh adhesive applied

INSTALLATION :

    Surface Preparation:
  1. All substrates receiving TSA ST + TSA PR must be flat, mechanically strong, free from loose parts, grease, oil, paint, wax, etc.

    2. Mixing ratio :
  2. The mixing ratio is determined by the degree of deformability required of the adhesive; use LTX133 with TSA PR or as a complete substitute for water with TSA ST when a highly deformable adhesive (class S2 according to EN 12004) is required.
  3. LTX133 diluted 1:1 with water with TSA ST may be used when a deformable adhesive is required (class S1 according to EN 12004).

    4. Application :
  4. Please refer to the TDS of respective adhesive with which LTX-133 is to be mixed.

    5. Installing the tiles

    u>
  5. Follow the recommendations shown in the technical data sheet of the adhesive with which LTX133 is mixed.
  6. Greater attention should be paid to the open time which, in the equivalent relative temperature and humidity conditions, will be slightly shorter than the open time of the basic product
